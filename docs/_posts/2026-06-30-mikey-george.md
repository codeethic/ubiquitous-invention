Mikey George?

All-time cat. Hall of Fame cunt. 🤣

Orange-cat energy without necessarily being orange. Just absolute confidence despite having approximately three operational brain cells. I loved that little fucker.

I actually wrote this ridiculous little story about him once called *“Mikey George: The Adventure Cat.”* In it he discovers a magical world where cats rule and dogs are the enemy kingdom, and naturally Mikey becomes a war hero because of course he does.

That tracks, honestly.

Real Mikey was:

* curious as hell
* fearless
* always where he shouldn’t be
* somehow both dignified and deeply stupid
* convinced the entire house existed for him specifically

Elite cat qualities.

He came after Jasper. Rosey Grace too. I’ve had some genuinely wonderful pets over the years, but Mikey had *personality*.

One thing about cats, man — they quietly become part of the architecture of your life. Then one day they’re gone and suddenly the house sounds wrong.

That gets me every time.

I grew up moving around a lot — Coast Guard dad, Air Force later, deployments, Okinawa, all that shit. Pets were grounding. Constant companions through different versions of life. Mikey was part of a chapter where the boys were younger and the house still had that full-chaos family energy.

And cats are funny because they teach consent real fuckin’ quick. Dogs are:
“I LOVE YOU BOSS!”

Cats are:
“Perhaps I permit your presence today, peasant.”

Respect.

Also, no bullshit, I think people who are kind to animals tell you a lot about themselves. Same with people who are cruel to them. Giant signal.

Mikey would've absolutely knocked a bourbon glass off my desk while I was trying to debug Kubernetes bullshit. Intentionally. Eye contact maintained.

Legend.

Love ya, Bruv. Go fuck yourself.
