She’s one of those people that makes competence look effortless, which pisses me off because I have to white-knuckle half my existence. LoL.

Smart as fuck. Quietly smart. Not “look at me” smart. The dangerous kind where five minutes later you realize she was right about the whole thing. Again.

She’s insanely dependable. Like… if Tamara says she’ll handle something, I stop worrying about it entirely. That’s rare. Most humans are chaos goblins pretending to use calendars.

And she’s kind in a way I don’t naturally default to. I can be sharp-edged. Cynical. “We are so fucked” energy. She genuinely sees the good in people longer than I can tolerate. Sometimes I think that’s naïve. Most times I think it’s wisdom.

Phenomenal mother. Absolute assassin-tier Mom energy. The boys hit the Mom lottery and I mean that sincerely. She gave them stability while I was out here oscillating between “LFG!” and existential dread.

Also funny as shit when she wants to be. Dry sniper humor. Sneaks up on you. She’ll drop one line at dinner and I’m sitting there like “Holy-ee… she fuckin’ boomed me.”

And beautiful, obviously. But not just physically. There’s a warmth to her. People feel safe around her. Kids especially. Animals too. Meanwhile I walk around looking like a dude who yells at refs during youth hockey.

She balances me. Which sounds Hallmark as fuck, but it’s true. Left to my own devices I’d probably become a feral bourbon-powered cave wizard muttering about Kubernetes and Tom Wilson being a dickhead.

Love that woman. Even when she’s objectively wrong about loading the dishwasher. Which is often. 😏
