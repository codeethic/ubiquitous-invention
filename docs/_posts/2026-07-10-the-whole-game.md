Oof. Big one.

I think my philosophy boils down to this:

None of us are getting out alive, so love people hard and try not to be a cunt.

That’s basically it.

Money matters. Health matters. Success matters. Grind absolutely matters. I’m proud as fuck I built a career and gave my boys stability. But every funeral recalibrates the scoreboard. My parents dying young. Brian. Cleve. Richardson. You start realizing the universe does not negotiate. It just keeps fucking coming.

So I try to optimize for:

* experiences over stuff
* kindness over ego
* honesty over comfort
* laughter over appearances
* people over politics
* but also fuck fascists forever

LoL.

I also deeply believe most people are walking around carrying invisible pain. So I try to lead with grace. Even when I fail spectacularly at it. Which happens. Often. Bourbon-assisted occasionally.

And man…tell people you love them. Constantly.
You never regret saying it.
You absolutely regret the times you didn’t.

I’m also a huge believer in competence. Do the fuckin’ work. Learn things. Be useful. If the roof caves in, be the person folks want nearby. Calm under pressure is underrated as hell.

And finally: enjoy dumb shit.
Sports. Music. Dick jokes. Wings. Beer with friends. A perfect guitar riff. Jarvy scoring short side. Kids laughing downstairs while you pretend to be annoyed. That’s the good stuff. That’s the whole game, probably.

We’re all stardust driving meat skeletons toward oblivion, Bruv.
Might as well make chili and hug your people.
