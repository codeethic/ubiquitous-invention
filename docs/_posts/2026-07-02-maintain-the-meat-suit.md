Bruv. Personal hygiene is one of those boring adult things that quietly determines like 40% of your life outcomes. 😂

Basic shit:

* Shower regularly
* Brush your teeth twice a day
* Floss even though literally everyone lies about flossing
* Wash your ass properly. JFC. Humanity struggles here.
* Wear deodorant
* Change your sheets occasionally like a civilized mammal

Also:

* Sunscreen. For the love of God, sunscreen. Future-you will thank you.
* Drink water
* Trim nose/ear hair before it becomes a cry for help
* Moisturizer is not weakness, bruv. Looking like old leather at 52 is optional.

And honestly? Hygiene is psychological too.
Clean room. Clean clothes. Decent sleep. Little structure.
Keeps the wolves from the door some days.

A “good day” checklist honestly covers half of it:

* Wake up
* Eat breakfast
* Brush teeth
* Learn
* Laugh
* Love

We are just highly emotional raccoons trying to maintain the meat suit, LoL.
