Because bouncers are basically the worst possible people to pick a fight with.

They get paid to deal with drunk, unpredictable assholes every weekend. They've seen every fake tough guy routine. They know how to control people, they've usually got 50-100 pounds on you, and they're almost never alone.

Best case? You wake up on the sidewalk with a bruised ego.

Middle case? You catch an assault charge, get banned from the bar, and spend a few thousand bucks explaining yourself to a judge.

Worst case? You hit your head on concrete, or one of his coworkers joins in, or somebody pulls a weapon. People have died over stupid bar fights.

And here's the kicker: even if you somehow "win," you still lose. You're now the guy who fought a bouncer. Congratulations. Enjoy the paperwork.

The smartest move is boring as fuck:

* Get tossed.
* Call the bouncer a cunt in your head.
* Go eat tacos.
* Wake up without a concussion or a criminal record.

That's a much better story the next day.
