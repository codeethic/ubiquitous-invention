Fuuuuuck, Bruv.

Playoff overtime hockey is the purest sport on earth.
No clock management. No load management. No fucking commercials every 90 seconds while some gambling app tells you to ruin your mortgage. Just exhaustion, violence, skill, and one mistake ending a season.

You can literally feel the cortisol through the TV.
Every dump-in feels like a heart attack. Every line change is chaos. Goalie standing on his god damn head. Defensemen playing 38 minutes looking like Civil War survivors.

And the handshake line after?
Still gets me. Every fuckin’ time.

Regular season OT? Fine. Cute little 3-on-3 pond hockey carnival.
Playoff OT is religion for atheists.

Nothing touches it.
Not NFL playoffs.
Not March Madness.
Certainly not NBA dudes taking 14 seconds to complain about a foul.

When the Canes are in OT I become completely unusable as a human being. Pacing. Sweating. Texting Corey every 11 seconds:
“JFC.”
“CLEAR THE FUCKING ZONE.”
“FREDDIE MY GOD.”
“LFG.”

Then Jarvy bangs one home and I’m yelling loud enough to scare the neighbors.

Bananas.
